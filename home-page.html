FROM THE PRESIDENT

RTR. MANAS SHETTY

Welcome to the Thane Royals Club. As President, my vision for this year is simple bring people together, strengthen our community spirit, and create experiences that leave a lasting impact. The Royals stand for commitment, collaboration, and growth, and I’m proud to lead a team that truly embodies these values. With every project, initiative, and small act of service, we hope to build a club that feels inclusive, vibrant, and driven by purpose. Thank you for being a part of this journey and for helping us take the Royals legacy forward.
